Quick Setup Guide

  1. Configure your IMAP settings above with your email provider details
  2. Click "Test Connection" to verify your settings work
  3. Save your configuration
  4. Go back to the inbox and click "Sync Now" to fetch your first emails
  5. Automatic sync will run every 5 minutes (or your configured interval)
